1. Introduction

At **Tidyzenic**, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your data when you interact with our website, apps, and services.

Transparency, security, and respect are core to our data practices. We are committed to protecting your information and being clear about how we use it.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone, payment details, and business information.
  • Usage Data: Information on your interactions with our platform, such as bookings and customer communication logs.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device details.
  • Cookies: Used to improve performance and personalize your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Securely manage and maintain your account.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third parties in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted partners for hosting, payment processing, analytics, and email communication under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Disclosures: Only when required by law or a valid government request.
  • Business Changes: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, your data may be transferred as a business asset.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. Your rights include the ability to:

  • Access and update your personal data at any time.
  • Request deletion or restriction of your data.
  • Request a copy of your data (portability).
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing.
  • File a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

6. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption, secure storage, and strict access controls. While no system is 100% secure, we are committed to minimizing risks and reacting quickly to any potential threats.

7. Children’s Privacy

Tidyzenic services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.
