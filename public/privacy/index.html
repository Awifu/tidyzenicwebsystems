Privacy Policy

How TidyZenic collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At TidyZenic, we are dedicated to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our services.

For more information on data protection, visit the official GDPR website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, company, email, phone, payment info when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps.
  • Service Data: Client details, bookings, employee schedules, and platform entries.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Personalize and improve user experience.
  • Process payments and manage accounts.
  • Communicate about updates and features.
  • Send marketing communications (you may opt-out).
  • Analyze platform usage to enhance features.

Data Security

We implement strong security measures to safeguard your data. While no online service is 100% secure, we work continuously to protect your information.

Sharing Your Information

We never sell or trade your personal data. We may share information only when:

  • Working with trusted providers (e.g., payments, hosting).
  • Complying with legal obligations.
  • In the event of a merger or acquisition.

Your Choices

  • Review and update your account information anytime.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails with one click.

Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
