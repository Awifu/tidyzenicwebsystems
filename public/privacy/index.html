Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 19, 2025

1. Introduction

At TidyZenic, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your data when you interact with our website, apps, and services.

Transparency, security, and respect are core to our data practices.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, email, phone, payment details, and business info.

  • Device Info: IP address, browser type, OS, screen size.
  • Cookies: Used to improve performance and personalize experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and maintain your account securely

  • To prevent abuse and ensure compliance

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With service providers: Hosting, payment, analytics, and email platforms under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal disclosures: Only when required by law or valid authority
  • Business changes: In the event of mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal data at any time
  • Request deletion or restriction of your data
  • Request a copy of your data (portability)
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

6. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption, secure storage, and access control.

While no system is 100% secure, we are committed to minimizing risks and reacting quickly to threats.

7. Children’s Privacy

TidyZenic services are not intended for users under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, please contact us immediately.
