The Story Behind TidyZenic

TidyZenic was founded by **Awifu Muyomba**, a passionate developer in Greece. Frustrated by the inefficiencies of managing service businesses, Awifu set out to build a comprehensive platform that would automate the tedious tasks and allow entrepreneurs to focus on what they do best. What started as a solo project has grown into a powerful suite of tools designed to streamline your operations, save you time, and boost your profitability.

Every feature, from AI-assisted scheduling to intuitive staff management, is built with the sole purpose of making your life easier and your business smarter.
