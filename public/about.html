About TidyZenic

Founded in Greece by Awifu Muyomba — a cleaning professional and Information Technology graduate — TidyZenic is a SaaS platform designed to empower service providers worldwide with AI-driven tools for growth, efficiency, and success.

Our Founder’s Story

After working in the cleaning industry for over 5 years, Awifu identified common challenges service providers face — from scheduling issues to inventory management.

With a background in Information Technology and over 14 years of international experience living abroad, Awifu merged his skills in technology with his passion for service. Partnering with other entrepreneurs, he launched a successful cleaning company, which became the foundation for TidyZenic.

Harnessing the power of AI, relentless research, and continuous feedback, Awifu built TidyZenic from scratch to help service providers thrive remotely and create sustainable livelihoods.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who founded TidyZenic?

TidyZenic was founded by Awifu Muyomba, an IT graduate and experienced cleaning professional with over 5 years of hands-on industry experience.

Where is TidyZenic based?

TidyZenic was founded in Greece and operates globally as a SaaS platform for service providers.

What does TidyZenic do?

TidyZenic provides AI-powered SaaS tools for service providers to manage scheduling, staff, inventory, and marketing with efficiency.

How does TidyZenic help service providers?

By automating scheduling, streamlining operations, and optimizing marketing, TidyZenic helps providers save time and increase profitability.
