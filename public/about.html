About TidyZenic

Founded in Greece by Awifu Muyomba — a cleaning professional and IT graduate — TidyZenic empowers service providers globally with AI-driven SaaS tools for growth, efficiency, and success.

Our Story

After 5 years in the cleaning industry, Awifu Muyomba experienced firsthand the challenges service providers face — from scheduling conflicts to client communication.

As an Information Technology graduate from Uganda, with 14 years of international experience abroad, Awifu combined his expertise in tech with his passion for service to create TidyZenic.

Built from scratch through research, coding, and continuous adaptation, TidyZenic is a platform that listens, evolves, and empowers businesses everywhere.
