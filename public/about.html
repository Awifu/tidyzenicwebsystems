About TidyZenic

Founded in Greece by Awifu Muyomba — a cleaning professional and IT graduate — TidyZenic empowers service providers globally with AI-driven SaaS tools for growth, efficiency, and success.

Our Story

From Cleaning to Code

With 5 years in the cleaning industry, Awifu Muyomba saw firsthand the challenges service providers face — from scheduling conflicts to staff management.

As an IT graduate from Uganda with 14 years of international experience abroad, Awifu merged his technical expertise and entrepreneurial drive to create TidyZenic.

“TidyZenic was born out of necessity — created for service providers, by someone who has lived their challenges.”
