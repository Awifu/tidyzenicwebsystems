Role‑Based Access Control to Secure Your Team

Assign roles like Admin, Manager, or Viewer—and fine-tune their permissions for safe, efficient team management.

How Role‑Based Access Works

  • Define roles (e.g., Admin, Manager, Viewer) and assign permissions like booking control, billing access, or analytics viewing.
  • Group users under roles instead of assigning permissions individually—streamlining your team's access rights.
  • Roles enforce the principle of least privilege—only granting access needed for each role.

Why This Matters

  • Enhanced security and minimized risk of unauthorized access. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Faster management with consistent permissions—ideal during onboarding and offboarding.
  • Improved auditability and regulatory compliance thanks to clear access logs.
  • Scalable as your team grows—easily adjust roles and permissions over time.

Implementing It Effectively

  • Start by modeling core roles and defining permission sets for each.
  • Create hierarchical roles that inherit permissions—e.g., a Manager inherits Viewer access plus additional tools. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
  • Regularly review role assignments to match evolving team structure or responsibilities.
