Storefront Manager — Sell Products & Services Elevated

Launch a smart, branded storefront to sell both physical products and services—equipped with inventory control, orders, and payment tools.

Key Capabilities

  • Create and manage both products and services in one storefront.
  • Track inventory, set low‑stock alerts, and sync across locations.
  • Manage orders—from placement to shipping—in a unified flow.
  • Integrate payment systems for seamless checkout and order fulfillment.

Why It Matters

  • Turn your site into a full-featured commerce hub—sell products alongside services without separate platforms.
  • Optimize time and costs—customers browse, checkout, and you manage everything from a streamlined dashboard.
  • Real-time insights—monitor stock, order flow, and revenue from one unified view.

How to Get Started

  1. Enable Storefront Manager in your dashboard settings.
  2. Add products and services, set pricing, descriptions, and inventory levels.
  3. Configure payment options and shipping or access rules.
  4. Publish your storefront and monitor orders and inventory in real time.
