What Does “White Label” Mean?

A white-label solution lets you rebrand and resell our product as if it were built in-house, hiding all traces of the original provider—including logos, domains, and color schemes.

Why Agencies Love It

  • Launch a full-featured platform fast—without building from scratch.
  • Keep your core brand consistent while expanding services.
  • Increase revenue via rebranded subscriptions under your name.
  • Maintain client trust—platform appears as your own development.

Setup Made Simple

  • Launch quickly: Publish the branded platform instantly.
