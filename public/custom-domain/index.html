Setup Is Simple

  • Enter Your URL: Input your preferred domain or subdomain directly in settings.

  • Automatic SSL: We handle SSL certificate generation, so your domain stays secure.
  • Brand Continuity: The entire booking flow stays on your domain—no redirects.

Why Use a Custom Domain?

  • Strengthen credibility with a familiar, brand-aligned URL.
  • Improve SEO and client trust by staying on your own domain.
  • Maintain session consistency—no mixed content or cookie issues.
  • Stand out with a fully white-labeled solution.
